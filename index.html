Named after Milton Reynolds (1892-1976), entrepreneur and manufacturer of the first ball point pen, the first Reynolds pen debuted in New York at the 32nd Street store on the morning of October 29, 1945. The day the pen went on sale, an estimated 5,000 shoppers stormed the shop, and approximately 50 NYPD officers had to be dispatched for crowd control.

Beginning with the debut in 1945 of its iconic 045 writing pen, Reynolds has continued to provide high quality writing instruments to consumers globally. Today, Reynolds is one of the most well-known brands in the Indian market with fabulous top-of-mind awareness and carries various much-in-demand products that include Trimax, Racer Gel, Jetter Classic, Brite and Champ. Reynolds Pens are now the favourite pens among millions in India and around the world.

An original is forever

Reynolds is an icon in itself, redefining precision and smoothness in writing. It has helped craft many a first drafts, winning proposals and out-of-the-box ideas. The original seal is the proof of the authenticity of the product as well as a reassurance of its premium quality. So, when purchasing your Reynolds pen, we recommend that you look for the original seal to enjoy the iconic Reynolds writing experience.
